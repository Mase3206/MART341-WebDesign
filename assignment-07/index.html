Assignment 7 — Comparing Bears

When I think of bears, I always think of brown, grizzly, and polar bears. Even though they are all bears, they definitely have their differences. Let's compare them!

Features Black Bear Grizzly Bear Polar Bear
Fur color Black Brown, with blonde highlights White, beige
Ears Pointed Rounded Rounded
Nose Pointed Pointed Conical
Face Smaller, rounder Wider, flatter Longer, conical
Paws Short claws Long claws Paddle-like paw
Shoulder hump No Yes No
Diet Berries, plant shoots, and nuts Roots, berries, insects, and fish Meat, primarily seals
