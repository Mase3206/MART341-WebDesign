Welcome!

Welcome to the TrustyCentre project page! Here you can find information about what the TrustyCentre is, what software it encapsualtes, what OSes and hardware it runs on, and how everything is bound together by the power of networking.

What is the TrustyCentre?

The TrustyCentre is a collection of related projects running on my "Trusty" cluster. Trusty is a small cluster of three Lenovo ThinkCentre M900 microcomputers running Rocky Linux as its OS and Docker as its containerization and clustering hypervisor. I named the cluster this simply because it sounds nice. "TrustyCentre" is thus a stylized portmanteau of "Trusty cluster" and "ThinkCentre", hence the European spelling.

Nerdiness

This is an incredibly nerdy project. I am repurposing old computers to do things that the vast majority of people either don't care about, didn't know was possible, or both. I am teaching myself how to do some things I wouldn't have dreamt of just a few years ago. The end goal, to use it as part of my HomeLab, is one that many of my coworkers fully understand the appeal — and I work in IT.

On top of that, have these fonts seemed at all recognizable to you? Unless you are a fellow tech nerd, probably not, as they are the fonts used in the Lenovo and ThinkPad logos. The ThinkPad font, Haettenschweiler, is not quite perfect though, as the version used in the ThinkPad and ThinkCentre logos has a red, circular dot above the "I" instead of a square.\

I'm totally not a Lenovo fanboy.
